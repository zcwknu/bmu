A little about me

Welcome to my personal page; I'm Brendan Unikewicz! I'm a Ph.D. student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology working with Professor Tal Cohen and colleagues at the Nonlinear Solid Mechanics Lab. For most of my career, I pioneered small business innovation efforts within our oceans by generating novel undersea technologies and pursuing grants that enabled exploratory device development. Now, I'm transforming medical technologies by creating a minimally invasive thyroid cancer diagnostic tool that is based on volume controlled cavity expansion. This digital palpation technology will revolutionize the current standard of preoperative care patients receive and reduce existing healthcare morbidities. Check back often and don't hesitate to reach out!
